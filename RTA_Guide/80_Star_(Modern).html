RTA Guide/80 Star Modern
80 Star Modern is like 80 Star Legacy but with different rules, legacy was the no major glitches version of 80 Star and Modern remove the rule of not doing major glitches, meaning that you can enter the first 2 bowser stages without having the required amount of star. However, clipping through the 80 Star door is still banned, meaning that you need 80 Stars before entering Bowser's Rainbow Rumble
