RTA Guide/0 Star HyperJump
0 Star HyperJump is the fastest category of Super Mario Star Road and is by far, the hardest category in the hack. In this category you do 1 major glitch calld "HyperJump" which makes you able to skip the first 2 Bowser stages. Making it possible to finish the game with 0 Key and in under 3 minutes.
